PROJECT: 04_SVM_SUP
TITLE: Data Sources & Feature Engineering

--------------------------------------------------
OVERVIEW:
This dataset contains 1,247 employee records from a manufacturing HR system across three site departments. The ML task is binary classification to identify high-risk employees (high_risk = 1) showing behavioral and operational patterns consistent with disengagement or performance decline risk, with a 30.7% high-risk rate. Source: simulated workforce data reflecting realistic manufacturing HR feature distributions.

--------------------------------------------------
REAL-WORLD DATA SOURCES:

Feature                 | Source System              | Instrument
------------------------|----------------------------|--------------------------
punctuality_rate        | Attendance / Time Tracking System | Badge reader or time-clock system — attendance punctuality ratio
engagement_score        | Internal Engagement Survey | Self-reported survey platform — periodic engagement score
productivity_index      | Production Performance System | MES / production tracking — employee output ratio
scrap_associated_pct    | Quality Tracking / Operator Log | QMS — scrap percentage attributed to this operator
training_hours_annual   | Training & Development Records | LMS — annual training hours completed
experience_yrs          | HR Records                 | HR master data — years in current role
area_rotation_rate      | HR / Workforce Movement Tracking | HR system — frequency of area assignment changes
department              | HR Master Data             | HR system — department assignment
shift                   | HR Master Data / Work Schedules | HR system — assigned work shift
contract_type           | HR Master Data             | HR system — contract category (Permanent / Temporary)
high_risk               | Derived Classification     | HR analytics — risk label based on behavioral and operational patterns

--------------------------------------------------
VARIABLE DESCRIPTIONS:

punctuality_rate       - Attendance punctuality ratio; low values reflect discipline issues that disrupt operational flow.
engagement_score       - Self-reported engagement level; most actionable lever for HR (LinearSVC coefficient -0.205).
productivity_index     - Direct efficiency indicator; links employee behavior to operational output.
scrap_associated_pct   - Scrap percentage linked to this operator; connects human behavior to quality outcomes. Coefficient +0.170.
training_hours_annual  - Annual training investment (hours); higher training reduces risk (coefficient -0.177).
experience_yrs         - Years of experience; improves consistency and decision-making.
area_rotation_rate     - Frequency of area rotation; excessive rotation may increase variability and learning curve effects.
department             - Categorical: Administration (41.6% high-risk), Quality (20.9%), Logistics, Maintenance.
shift                  - Categorical: Night shift is the strongest structural risk factor (coefficient +0.249).
contract_type          - Categorical: Temporary (40.6% high-risk) vs Permanent (25.7%).
high_risk              - Binary classification target: 1 = patterns consistent with disengagement or performance decline risk, 0 = low risk.

--------------------------------------------------
FEATURE ENGINEERING:

StandardScaler applied to numerical features. OneHotEncoder (drop_first) applied to categorical features (department, shift, contract_type). All inside a sklearn Pipeline. GridSearchCV scoring on F1; best: kernel=rbf, C=50, gamma='scale'. A LinearSVC companion model trained alongside for coefficient-level interpretability. Train/Test split: 75/25, stratified, random_state=42.

--------------------------------------------------
BUSINESS CONTEXT:

Used by HR business partners and operational excellence managers in manufacturing environments to enable early-warning risk scoring. A high score flags an employee for a retention conversation — not a verdict — at the right time before disengagement manifests in performance or turnover.

--------------------------------------------------
GOAL:

Identify employees with high operational risk based on behavioral and operational patterns to improve retention, reduce scrap from human factors, and enable proactive HR intervention. Model achieved Accuracy 68.9%, AUC-ROC 0.666, Recall (High Risk) 45.8%.
