PROJECT: 09_Lasso_SUP
TITLE: Data Sources & Feature Engineering

--------------------------------------------------
OVERVIEW:
This dataset contains 1,843 weld bead records captured from a MIG/MAG welding cell PLC, inline sensors, and post-weld inspection logs. The ML task is regression to predict weld penetration depth (%) from 17 candidate features, using Lasso to simultaneously perform feature selection (6 of 17 variables zeroed by L1 penalty). Target range: 0.0–94.5%, mean 48.8%, adequate zone [60–90%]: 24.4% of welds.

--------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------
VARIABLE DESCRIPTIONS:

voltage_v              - Arc voltage (V); controls arc geometry and weld pool profile. Coefficient +3.78 (active).
current_a              - Welding current (A); primary energy driver — amperage determines heat input per unit length. Coefficient +9.14 — strongest positive driver.
wire_feed_speed_mm_s   - Wire feed rate (mm/s); secondary energy driver — more wire means more filler and arc energy. Coefficient +7.84.
material_temp_c        - Base material preheat temperature (°C); marginal contribution (active).
ambient_temp_c         - Ambient temperature in welding bay (°C); marginal contribution (active).
travel_speed_mm_s      - Torch travel speed (mm/s); active with marginal contribution.
torch_angle_deg        - Torch inclination angle (°); ZEROED — angle variation within operating range carries no independent signal.
ctwd_mm                - Contact tip to work distance (mm); longer distance reduces current density at arc. Coefficient -0.26 (active).
gas_flow_l_min         - Shielding gas flow rate (L/min); adequate flow prevents oxidation and porosity. Coefficient +0.83 (active).
co2_pct                - CO2 content in shielding gas mix (%); ZEROED — captured by gas_flow_l_min at observed conditions.
thickness_mm           - Base plate thickness (mm); thicker plate absorbs more energy. Coefficient +1.09 (active).
steel_type             - Steel grade category (0, 1, 2); ZEROED — grade categories do not separate penetration outcomes in this dataset.
rust_level             - Surface rust score 0–10 from vision system; strongest negative driver. Coefficient -2.61.
surface_grease         - Surface contamination score 0–10 from vision system; second negative driver. Coefficient -2.15.
wire_diameter_mm       - Wire electrode diameter (mm); ZEROED — diameter held nearly constant in this dataset.
roller_wear_pct        - Wire feeder roller wear (%); ZEROED — wear variation within range has no independent effect on penetration.
arc_stability_rms      - Arc voltage RMS fluctuation; ZEROED — captured by other electrical parameters.
weld_penetration_pct   - Effective weld penetration depth (%); regression target.

--------------------------------------------------
FEATURE ENGINEERING:

StandardScaler applied to all 17 features — mandatory for Lasso so the L1 penalty applies fairly across features on different scales. LassoCV evaluated 60 alpha candidates on log scale from 0.001 to 100 via 5-fold CV; selected alpha = 0.1081. Result: 11 active features, 6 zeroed. OLS on all 17 features achieves the same R² = 0.847, confirming zeroed variables carry no independent signal. Train/Test split: 80/20, random_state=42.

--------------------------------------------------
BUSINESS CONTEXT:

Used by welding process engineers and quality teams in structural fabrication lines. The contamination finding — rust and grease are the two strongest negative drivers — provides a directly quantifiable business case for mandatory pre-weld cleaning steps that is visible to anyone who reads the model output.

--------------------------------------------------
GOAL:

Predict weld penetration depth and perform feature selection via L1 regularization to identify the 11 critical process drivers, eliminate monitoring of 6 redundant variables, and quantify the impact of surface contamination on weld quality. Model achieved R² = 0.847, RMSE = 6.27 percentage points, MAE = 5.08 percentage points.
